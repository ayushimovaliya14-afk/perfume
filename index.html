Common Enquiries

How long does the scent last?
Ayushi solid perfumes are concentrated at 25-30% fragrance load. They evolve with your skin's warmth and typically provide 6-8 hours of beautiful sillage.
Are these travel friendly?
Absolutely. Since they are solid, they are TSA-friendly and spill-proof. They fit perfectly into pockets or small travel bags.
Sourcing & Sustainability
We use ethical beeswax and organic carrier oils. Handpicked for purity, bridging artisanal tradition with environmental consciousness.
